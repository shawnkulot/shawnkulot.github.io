CAFFÈ LATTE

SMALL

Caffè latte is a popular coffee drink made with a shot of espresso and steamed milk, typically topped with a small layer of frothy milk. It has a smooth, creamy texture and a mild coffee flavor, making it less intense than other espresso-based drinks like cappuccinos. The name "latte" means "milk" in Italian, reflecting the drink's emphasis on milk. It can be enjoyed plain or flavored with syrups like vanilla or caramel.
