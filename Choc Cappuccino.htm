CHOCOLATE CAPPUCCINO

SMALL

Chocolate cappuccino is a rich, creamy coffee beverage that combines the bold flavor of espresso with the sweetness of chocolate. It typically features steamed milk and a frothy milk foam layer, with chocolate syrup or cocoa powder blended in or drizzled on top, creating a harmonious balance between coffee and chocolate. This indulgent drink is often enjoyed as a dessert or a cozy pick-me-up.
