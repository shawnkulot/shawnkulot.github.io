CARAMEL MACCHIATO

TALL

Caramel macchiato is a popular espresso-based coffee drink that features a shot of espresso poured over steamed milk, sweetened with vanilla syrup, and topped with a drizzle of caramel sauce. It has a layered taste, combining the boldness of espresso, the creaminess of milk, and the sweetness of caramel, making it a favorite for those who enjoy both coffee and dessert-like flavors.
