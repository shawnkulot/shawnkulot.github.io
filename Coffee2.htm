BREWED COFFE

TALL

Brewed coffe is a classic coffee preparation method that involves extracting flavors from ground coffee beans using hot water. This process can be achieved through various techniques, such as drip brewing, French press, or pour-over. Brewed coffee typically offers a rich, aromatic flavor profile and can be enjoyed black or with added milk, cream, or sweeteners. Its versatility and ease of preparation make it a popular choice for coffee lovers around the world.
