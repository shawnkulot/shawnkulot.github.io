FLAT WHITE

TALL

Flat white is a coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk, known for its smooth and velvety texture. It typically has a higher proportion of coffee to milk compared to a latte, resulting in a stronger coffee flavor. The milk in a flat white is microfoamed, giving it a creamy consistency without large bubbles, which enhances the rich, bold taste of the espresso. It originated in Australia or New Zealand and has gained popularity worldwide.
